🔔 OpenDKP Helper Settings

🎚️ Mode

Choose how the extension behaves. Raid Leader mode enables raid leader tools and stronger alerts. Raider mode focuses on personal alerts and smart bidding.

📁 RaidTick Integration

Search for RaidTick .txt files in your TAKP folder and copies the contents to your clipboard for easy OpenDKP import.

In game, type /outputfile raidlist to generate a RaidTick .txt file with your current raid.
(Requires Zeal.)

The helper window automatically closes after copying.

⏰ RaidTick Reminders

Up to 5 reminders. Each reminder triggers every 5 minutes between Start and End times on selected days. TTS reads your message if enabled.

⚔️ Loot Parser

Firefox restricts extensions from keeping live access to local files. To reliably parse loot: open the Loot Monitor window, choose your EQ log once, and leave the window open. It checks every few seconds and pushes new loot lines into the popup automatically.

In game, type your identifier before pressing 'Link Loot' (Zeal Feature with compatible UI). The parser will detect log lines containing your identifier and push the items to the popup.

Close the monitor window to stop; reopen it to resume. Nothing is uploaded—all processing stays local.

🔊 Audio Settings

70%
Choose your preferred notification sound. Custom sounds require uploading an audio file.

🗣️ Text-to-Speech

1.0x
Available variables:
{winner} - Winner name
{bidAmount} - Bid amount
{itemName} - Item name
{winners} - Comma-separated winners list
{isRollOff} - true/false
{multipleWinners} - true/false
Text-to-speech announces auction completions after the sound plays. Requires browser permission.

📢 Read New Auctions

Speaks "New auction: Item Name" when a new auction appears. Uses your TTS voice and speed settings. Respects Quiet Hours.

🗂️ Custom Sound Manager

Upload MP3, WAV, or OGG files. Maximum 3 custom sounds. Suggested size ≤ 100 KB each for quick notifications. Files are stored locally in your browser.

🧠 Smart Notifications

Only alerts when you win an auction (detects your characters from the header). Automatically enabled for Raider profile.

Smart mode only alerts for auctions you're bidding on. Quiet Hours disables sound notifications during the selected time range.

Reminds you to upload raid logs when you leave opendkp. Raid Leader mode only.

👁️ Visual Settings

Visual alerts help ensure you don't miss timer completions. Can be disabled globally.

⚙️ Advanced Settings

Adjust how often the extension checks for timer changes. Faster is more responsive; slower uses less CPU.
